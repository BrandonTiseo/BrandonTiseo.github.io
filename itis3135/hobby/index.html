What is Gardening?

Gardening is the activity that involves the care and cultivation of plants. Gardening can be done for various purposes including growing food, creating decoration, or providing wildlife habitats.

Being a gardener means you understand the care process required for your plants, as every plant can have very different needs from one another. Being a gardener is also not only applicable to people with lots of plants. If you only have one plant you take care of you are a gardener!

When Should I Garden?

Gardening can be done at any time of the year but which plants you can grow at which time varies. Some plants thrive in Spring but die in Winter and vice versa. Some plants do better outdoors and some indoors.

There is a lot of variation between the conditions each plant thrives in and it is important to know those conditions! I personally like to keep track of important care information for each of my plants which I can reference anytime!

How do I Garden?

You may be wondering "how do I even take care of my plants?" It is actually far more simple than you may think and only takes a handful of steps to ensure your plants are healthy! Below is a general guide on how to take care of your plants:

  • Place your plants in their ideal light level.
  • Give your plants their correct soil type and optionally some fertillizer.
  • Follow your plants water schedule to ensure they are always properly hydrated.
  • Occasionally repot the plant to ensure it has room to grow.
