Website Evaluations

Good Website Design

pinterest.com

Pinterest is a visual discovery engine for finding ideas like recipes, home and style inspiration, and more.

  • Contrast:

    Pinterest successfully fulfills the contrast design principle as it has an all white background with black and red, which easily stand out on the white background, as its two primary colors for icons and text.

  • Repetition:

    Pinterest successfully fulfills the repetition design principle. Each page contains consistant headers and footers for ease-of-use and familiarity. Furthermore, every page, regardless of its purpose, follows a similar design principle with everything being contained within rounded squares.

  • Alignment:

    Pinterest successfully fulfills the alignment design principle. All items on the page align vertically with each other. Furthermore, the image containers, regardless of their varying shapes and content, all stay relatively aligned with one another, keeping the design clean.

  • Proximity:

    Pinterest successfully fulfills the proximity design principle. No element on the page is right up against one another and there it consistant margins and padding around each one.

Poor Website Design

arngren.net

Arngren.net is one of Norway's oldest web shops. It was originally a mail order company prior to the internet.

  • Contrast:

    Arngren.net does not fulfill the contrast design principle. Many elements use similar colors, such as borders and text both being black. Furthermore, some elements have a green background with green text on it.

  • Repetition:

    Arngren.net does not fulfill the repetition design principle. There is various inconsistencies amongst the different page elements. Many of the shapes containing the images are different.Furthermore, many of the font's are all over the place with no consistency or indication of their meaning.

  • Alignment:

    Arngren.net does not fulfill the alignment principle as most elements on the page are not aligned in a specific way horizontally or vertically, with most being placed wherever and some overlapping.

  • Proximity:

    Arngren.net does not fulfill the proximity design principle as most elements are right next to eachother and some even overlap one another.
