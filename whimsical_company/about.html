About Us.

Bold Turtle is a small and passionate baking company. Our goal is to bring carefully crafted and bold flavors to every one of our baked goods.

We also bring a fun twist: turtles! We theme every one our items around turtles. We also donate a large portion of our profits towards turtle conservation.

Turtles are our biggest inspiration, and without them we would never be able to bring you the flavors you've come to love!
